The Receipts engine is a high-fidelity metabolic tracker that calculates your precise Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). By using moving averages and regression models, it filters out weight fluctuations from sodium, hydration, and glycogen. The system continuously updates your calorie and macronutrient targets based on your real-world progress. It acts as an automated coach to protect muscle mass, prevent stalls, and guarantee predictable body recomposition results without the usual guesswork.
